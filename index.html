About Horus AI

HorusAI is a Melbourne-based health tech startup dedicated to harnessing the power of Artifical Intelligence to improve medical diagnostics.

We strongly believe in global health and aim to bridge the inequalities in healthcare- to benefit developing and developed countries alike.

Our pioneer project focuses on malaria. 200 million cases of malaria were reported in 2015, with gross inequalities in the level of care available between malaria-endemic countries.

HorusAI uses machine learning software to bridge these gaps, replacing manual blood film analysis with an automated solution that takes a fraction of the time and achieves superior accuracy.
